Privacy Policy



WeTrace was inspired by the need to protect your privacy.

We do not collect any data except what is necessary to trace physical contacts. Contact data is anonymized.

All the tracing data remains on your phone, until you decide to share it with the rest of the world. You would be ask to do so if you test positive.

We verify your phone number so that we can reach you if someone you've been in contact with someone who tests positive.


For information or support, please contact @.
